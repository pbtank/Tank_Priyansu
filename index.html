About me!

I am currently pursuing my M.Tech. in Aerospace Engineering at Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore, India. I have done my Bachelors in Mechanical Engineering from VGEC, Ahmedabad, India. I also have a diploma in Mechanical Engineering from Government Polytechnic, Rajkot, India.

My main field of interests are Aerospace, specifically in satellite thermal and propulsion systems and in Mechanical, specifically vibration analysis of structures and simulations of fluid and heat transfer systems. I also have a bit experience in coding (As you might have guessed by looking at this portfolio!) with mathematical models, CFD and documentation in LaTeX. Go to my Projects tab for more details about my previous work and experiences. I prefer self learning everything, from Calculus to Quantum Mechanics!
